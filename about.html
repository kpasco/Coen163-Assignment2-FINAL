About Us

We are a small company based in North Yorkshire, specialising in the design, manufacture and sales of wooden doors stops, wood wedges, model stands, light pulls, trophy bases and other small handturned and other wooden items. We hold a small amount of stock, but some items are made to order; usually within 1 week. Large quatities may take a little longer, please ask first if large quantities are required within a short timescale.

Any of our designs can be specially made from almost any wood to suit your requirements. Please enquire via our contact us page if you require any special timbers, quantities, different designs etc. WoodenDoorStopShop do not currently charge any VAT. We always aim to provide a high quality product and service.
